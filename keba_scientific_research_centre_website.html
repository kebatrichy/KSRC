About Us

KEBA Scientific Research Centre, Trichy, is dedicated to promoting quality research and innovation in chemical, biological and interdisciplinary sciences. Since 2021, the centre has been providing academic guidance, hands-on training and research support to students and scholars.

Research Focus Areas

Green synthesis of nanoparticles (ZnO, CuO)
Antimicrobial and antioxidant studies
Microwave & ultrasound assisted organic synthesis
Biocatalysts and eco-friendly methodologies
